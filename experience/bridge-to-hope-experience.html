EXPERIENCE 04

Bridge to Hope

As Founder and Executive Director of Bridge to Hope, I led a student-run organization focused on educational workshops, community service, and support for children affected by poverty.

Role

Founder & Executive Director

What I Worked On

I founded the organization, recruited volunteers, planned workshops, coordinated outreach, and helped organize service initiatives. The organization grew to over 50 volunteers and focused on making educational resources more accessible.

Impact

Bridge to Hope hosted educational and interactive workshops and organized a city-wide food drive that helped feed over 300 children in Imperial County, California.

Skills Used

Leadership, Volunteer Recruitment, Program Planning, Community Outreach, Communication, Public Speaking, Event Organization, Social Impact

What I Learned

This experience taught me how to turn a mission into an organized initiative. I learned how to lead volunteers, communicate with community partners, and create programs that serve real needs.
